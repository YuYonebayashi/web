About Spain

The capital of Spain is Madrid. It has an area of 506,000㎢, which is about 1.3 times the size of Japan. The population is about 46.93 million. It is of Iberian-Celtic origin, and the present Spaniards consist of Castilians, Galicians, Andalusians, Catalans and Basques. It is a very popular country for tourists.
